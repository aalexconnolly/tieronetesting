Clive Slattery

Consultant

Clive Slattery is one of the country’s foremost pensions consultants. Clive worked in the Revenue for 32 years including the management of the Revenue’s Pensions Business Unit for 16 years. He provides detailed technical advice and support to TierOne on all pre and post retirement pension vehicles. This experience allows TierOne to deliver sophisticated tax efficient pensions solutions to our clients.
